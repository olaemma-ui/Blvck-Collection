Description

Introducing Shades of Blvck, our latest collection of essentials, available in six different shades. 

The Blvck Hoodie 'Back' boasts a black tone and an oversized fit.

The back features a bold 'Blvck Paris' logo in UV print and the front features a subtle logo in the chest in UV rubber print. 

Crafted from a luxurious blend of fleece cotton, it's impossibly soft to the touch and lends a perfect fit for whichever activity the day may bring.

This piece boasts an oversized fit.
